Hindi Pathshala

Download now file_download

Now learn Hindi in the most easy & fun manner for free !

Hindi Pathshala presented by Webmobic Technologies brings to you the easiest way to learn Hindi. Here you can start learning from the most basic elements of a language like Swar & Vyanjan. Once you have completed them, it also has higher level features like counting numbers, names of different days, months, fruits, vegetables, flowers, birds, animals, body parts & colours as well. After this you can also test yourself with the available quiz that analyzes your performance. Also it has a full version that is add free!

It guides you through the learning path in following steps:

  • Learn foundation principles of Hindi like Swar & Vyanjan.
  • Move to next level essentials of numeric counting
  • Start the groundlevel work by learning names of week days & months
  • Get to know the names of basic fruits, flowers & vegetables
  • Master the names of birds & animals with advanced phonetics
  • Easily memorize the names of various colours in Hindi
  • Familiarize yourself with Human body parts
  • Examine your skills with our wide quiz range

Hence it offers a wide range of study material for beginners & helps them learn Hindi in the most fun manner. Apart from the basic fonts , it also has audio & visual effects that help you identify the exact pronunciation of alphabets & pictures that make it easy to identify objects in daily life. Once you follow the complete track & have gained the insights successfully, you can opt for the panel game / quiz to check your grasping power & scrutinize your weak areas. So discover your true potential with Hindi Pathshala.
